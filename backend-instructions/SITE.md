## 1. Core Identity
* **Project Name:** Career Guidance Platform
* **Stitch Project ID:** `6260758564596494843`
* **Mission:** To digitize the manual career guidance journey into a premium, hybrid platform with structured modules, AI reporting, and human expert validation.
